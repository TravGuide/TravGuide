About TravGuide

We have just entered a new field, but we have seen most of the problems and solved them.

Welcome to our website that helps you explore five countries:- 1. Egypt 2. South Korea 3. Turkey 4. England 5. Malaysia The website consists of 6 sections: the main page, which contains the navigation bar that includes Home, About, countries, offers, or the package page, the gallery, and contact information. We will talk about the website pages, starting with the countries. When you click on the button on the card for each country, you will be transferred to another page that displays Tourism details in countries such as the types of tourism in the country and also the tourist places in each type of tourism and also at the end of the page you will find a registration form which consists of where you will go and also how many people and also the date of departure and also the date of return and at the end you will find a place for a large message in which you write the details of the trip and also your personal details Then this data is sent to a private tourism company that organizes the trip. Before the form, there are four pages for services. The first one is transportation. There are two types of transportation: renting a car and the second is renting product shipping vehicles. The second page has… Hotels: There are two hotels in each tourist location, and all the details you might need are available on this page in an easy way for the customer. The third page is the restaurants page. We have provided four restaurants next to each location. The page is responsive and easy to use. The fourth page is for banks, and this is its mission if any tourist in any of the five countries knows a place to exchange currency, etc. Finally, there is a page to contact us.
